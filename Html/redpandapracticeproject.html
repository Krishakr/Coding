All about Red Panda's


What they eat

Red Panda's usally feed on bamboo and leaves. They sometimes even snack on different fruits, insects, and small lizards.

Facts about Red Panda's

1. Red pandas are the first known species of pandas

2. A Red Panda can stand up like a human

3. Red Panda's glow in the dark

4. Red Panda's are a lot like cats and bears

5. Red Panda's are not related to Giant Panda's
